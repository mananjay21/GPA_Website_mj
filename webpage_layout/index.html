Goverment Polytechnic College Adityapur

Government Polytechnic, Adityapur is among one of the 18 Polytechnics in Jharkhand founded to groom the technical manpower and contribute to our state and country through excellence in scientific and technical education. It was established in the year 1980, by the Department of Science and Technology, Govt. of Bihar., to promote the academic growth among the tribal community in particular and to serve as a valuable resource for industry and society in general.
